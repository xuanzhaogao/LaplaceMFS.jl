LaplaceMFS.double_sphere_image_coefficients — Method
double_sphere_image_coefficients(r, d, Ez, eps_r; tol=1e-14, maxiter=200)

Image-reflection (method of reflections) dipole model for two identical dielectric spheres of radius r, center distance d along x, in a uniform field Ez * ẑ.

Returns (a1, a2), where each sphere contributes the scattered potential u_s(x) = a_s * z_local / |x-c_s|^3.
